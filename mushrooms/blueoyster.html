Blue Oyster

Pleurotus ostreatus

Appearance

Blue Oyster mushrooms begin with deep blue-gray caps that lighten to silver as they grow. They form layered clusters with broad, fan-shaped caps.

Texture and Flavor

They have silky caps and firm stems, offering a mild, earthy flavor. Blue Oysters brown beautifully and develop a rich, savory aroma when cooked.

Culinary Uses

Perfect for frying, roasting, stir-fries, soups, and noodle dishes. Their shape works well whole or sliced.

Additional Info

If product is dried, keep in a cool, dry place. If you wish to rehydrate, soak mixed mushrooms in warm water for 15 to 30 minutes depending on size and thickness.
